# Market Penetration Brief v0.10.66

`market_penetration_brief_v1` separates exact-date market observations, media
narratives, unsupported or stale repetitions, hypotheses, and verified facts.
RSS is display-only. A narrative is never a verified fact and does not enter
graph scoring. `verified_facts` is intentionally empty until a primary-source,
deterministic verification adapter is enabled.
